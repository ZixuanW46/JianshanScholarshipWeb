## 2. What’s “Jianshan”: Meaning, brand and mission
**Content:**
- **Subtitle:** About Jianshan
- **Description:** The name "Jianshan" (见山), which literally translates to "Seeing the Mountain," comes from a well-known Zen Buddhist parable of Zen master Qingyuan Weixin:
  > "First, we see mountains as mountains. Then, mountains are no longer mountains. Finally, mountains are truly mountains again."
  
  This parable describes a path to understanding: from a superficial view to critical questioning, and ultimately to a richer, more nuanced appreciation of the world.

**UI Design:**
- **Subtitle Style:** Tag-like style.
- **Layout:** Placed under the subtitle, using a split-screen layout with a picture alongside the text.

## 6. Options for Jianshan 2026
**Content:**
- We're expected to host 3 individual 7-day Jianshan Academies between July and August. These will take place in three of our five core hubs: Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen, Hangzhou, or Chengdu.
- While the camp locations vary, the experience of both the camp and the trip remains consistent. Every Jianshan Scholar participates in the same 11-day Capy China Trip—a signature immersive journey featuring Beijing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou.
- During the application, you will indicate your time availability and location preference to help us align the cohort. The final results will provide a confirmed location and date tailored to your schedule.

## 7. Application Process
**Content:**

### Application Timeline
- **1st round (Applications close on Mar 27)**
  - We'd love to bring interesting and passionate individuals to Jianshan Academy. Let us know about you.
  - Indicate your Jianshan Academy location preference and time availability.
  - Select as many date options as possible to increase your chances of admittance.
- **2nd round (Invitations sent on a rolling basis, usually within 7 days of your application)**
  - Shortlisted candidates show their thoughts on the design of academic sessions.
  - You will also be invited to submit a 1-3 minute video to introduce yourself and your sessions. No fancy editing needed! Just a way for us to see your communication style.
- **Result (by Apr 10)**
  - Successful scholars will receive their official invitation with confirmed camp details. The result will be updated in the Jianshan Academy portal and notified through email.
